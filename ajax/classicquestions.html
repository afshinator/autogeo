Here's some classic questions you can double-click to choose from, categorized by the house in which their answer will fall. You can also just use this list to get an idea which houses different questions would fall into.

House 1

By studying the meaning of each house and these questions, you will be able to determine what houses your own unique questions fall in.

NOTE: There is a basic assumption that you are doing the divination, and for yourself. If you're asking for someone else, or asking about someone else's life, the house numbers shift based on the relationship you have to the person you're asking for.
