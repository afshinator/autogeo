Populus (People) - Multitude, diffussion, the crowd

- Multitude, a gathering or assembly of people, or a large amount of anything. Takes on the qualities of figures it interacts with.

- A figure of dispersal and multiplicty (as opposed to Via). Passive, reflective, indirect action.

- A stable but passive figure that takes on the qualities of the figures that interact with it.

Favorable: in matters requiring quiet reflection. Fortunate in the company of other fortunate figures.

Unfavorable: in matters demanding focused action. Unfortunate in the company of other unfortunate figures.

Stable, Moon, Cancer, Outer: Water, Inner: Water, the breasts and midriff

Laetitia (Joy) - Happiness and health.

- Very positive figure, representing upward movement. A figure of fortitude.

Favorable: for most questions. (examples: career, emotional state, health)

Unfavorable: in questions where stability and deep roots are needed; very unfavorable when a secret needs to be kept

Mobile, Jupiter, Pisces, Outer: Water, Inner: Fire, the feet

Rubeus (Red) - Passion, Anger

- Passion, power, fierceness, and vice.

- Passion and involvement in life, balancing the abstract detachment of Albus

Favorable: in questions involving sexuality, intoxicant, and violence!

Unfavorable: in everything else!

Mobile, Mars, Scorpio, Outer: Water, Inner: Air, the genitals and reproductive system

Fortuna Minor (Lesser Fortune) - Transitory Success

- A figure of Outer strength and success, easily gained and just as easily lost.

- Success coming from outside help rather than from within (in the case of Fortuna Major).

Favorable: when rapid change is expected, or you want to proceed quickly

Unfavorable: when fickleness or instability is a problem.

Mobile, Sun, Leo, Outer: Air, Inner: Fire, the spine,

Albus (White) - Peace and Detachment

- peace, wisdom, purity, detachment; A generally favorable but weak figure

- awareness caught up in inner life, turned away from action in outer world; \ chastity

Favorable: for beginnings, for business ventures, for matters requiring intelligence and quiet progress

Unfavorable: for matters requiring courage and energy, or in situations involving conflict or disruptive change

Stable, Mercury, Gemini, Outer: Air, Inner: Water, the shoulders and lungs

Amissio (Loss) - Transience and Loss

- Transience and loss, things outside one's grasp, also a figure of generosity!

Favorable: for love (lose your heart). Good for any situation where loss is desired (losing your heart, fears, sickness, problems)!

Unfavorable: for material matters. Unfavorable whenever you hope to gain something from the situation.

Mobile, Venus, Taurus, Outer: Earth, Inner: Fire, the neck and throat,

Conjunctio (Conjunction) - Contact, Union, Interaction

- Combination of forces or people; recovery of things that have been lost. Shows the presence of a combo of forces. Favorable or unfavorable depending on other figures.

- A figure of temperance, and a neutral figure, neither favorable nor unfavorable. represents the union of opposites on all levels and the resulting potentials for change.

- Tends to be favorable or unfavorable based on its interactions.

Favorable: for recovering lost or stolen things; for matters in which things need to come into contact

Unfavorable: for any situation requiring solitude, separation, and clarification

Mobile, Mercury, Virgo, Outter: Earth, Inner: Air, the instestines and belly

Cauda Draconis (Dragons Tail) - Endings, Completions, Disruptions

- Endings and letting go of the past. Disruptions and sometimes losses.

- Traditionally a figure of generosity!

Favorable for losses and ending only

Unfavorable in most other questions!

Mobile, South node of the Moon, Sagittarius, Outer: Fire, Inner: Fire, the left arm

Tristitia (Sorrow) - Sorrow and Difficulty

- Suffering, illness, and pain.

- Stands for downward movement. Lowered spirits, lowered vitality, lowerd expectations but also stability and solidity (sinking roots into ground)

Favorable: in questions relating to building and the Earth, and when a secret needs to be kept; things requiring stuckness or permanence

Unfavorable in most other matters!

Stable, Saturn, Aquarius, Outer: Air, Inner: Earth, the ankles,

Carcer (Prison) - Isolation and Restriction

- Restriction, isolation, always portends delay, binding, limitation, imprisonment, but also protection and focus

- can mean greed in financial questions

Favorable: anything requiring involving stability, security, and isolation

Unfavorable: most other things!

Stable, Saturn, Capricorn, Outer & Inner: Earth, the knees and lower legs

Acquisitio (Gain) - Gain and Success

- Success, profit, and gain; things desired are within one's grasp.

- A figure of prudence

Favorable : any situation where gaining something in desired

Unfavorable: when loss is desired (losing your fear, illness, your predicament)

Stable, Jupiter, Sagittarius, Outer: Fire, Inner: Air, the hips and thighs

Puer (Boy) -(male sexual) Energy, Force, Sudden Change

- Rashness, violence, passionate energy, destruciveness. Also justice.

- unstable and thoughtless force, it represents conflict, sudden change, seeking, and transformation, with all positive and negative consequences

- energy and purpose, interaction with others, a material expression but no receptive inner life

Favorable where energy, enthusiasm, courage, and change are desirable

Unfavorable in matters requiring prudence, maturity, and stability

Mobile, Mars, Aries, Outer:Air, Inner:Fire, the head,

Fortuna Major (Greater Fortune) - Inner strength and resulting Success

- Great good fortune and inner strength. A figure of power and success, that comes about without apparent effort

- Foretells good fortune, power and success that unfolds naturally, (but often with a hard start)

Favorable: almost all questions, especially for any competition,

Unfavorable: in situations involing escaping from a difficult situation

Stable, Sun, Leo, Outter: Fire, Inner: Earth, the heart and chest

Puella (Girl) - Harmonious, but ambivalent

- Harmony and happiness, but fickle, ambivalent;

- female sexual energy, lacks only relationship and interaction to be complete (whereas Puer seeks only to be received).

Favorable: most questions except where permanence is required; especially for love and friendship, and short-term happiness

Unfavorable: in situations where permanence is desired

Stable, Venus, Libra, Outer: Air, Inner: Water, the kidneys and lower back

Caput Draconis (Dragons Head) - Beginnings and Opportunities

- Beginnings and new possiblities; often foretells a positive outcome with tough beginnings

- Takes on favorable or unfavorable character of elements it interats with

Favorable for beginnings and gain

Unfavorable where ending something is desirable

Stable, North node of the Moon, Virgo, Outer: Earth, Inner: Earth, the right arm

Via (Way) - Directed Movement and Change

- change in all its forms, directed movement

Favorable: for all kind of journeys, physical and metaphorical; all questions in which change is an advantage

Unfavorable: when leaving things unchanged is desired

Mobile, Moon, Cancer, Outer & Inner: Water, the stomach
